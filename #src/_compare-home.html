How to compare the best antivirus software

While most antivirus programs have a lot to offer in terms of functionality, there are a few basic criteria you need to take into account when choosing the right solution. You can expect a good baseline antivirus protection system, regardless of what product you choose, but you should have a close look at them and compare their features and capabilities. You may want something fast, that doesn't slow down your computer or you may find a dedicated protection features for your sensitive documents more important, so compare the products and their features carefully, to find a good balance.

The first thing you need to look at, when comparing antivirus programs, is how well a program can detect viruses, intrusions or other dangers. The best antivirus programs on the market went through rigorous evaluations, conducted by independent testers, so you can be sure that their scores reflect the detection and removal capabilities quite well. Look closely and compare antivirus scores carefully, because a high score also means an accurate detection, which is quite important, because you don't want your frequently used applications to get marked as threats and removed by mistake, as the result of a false detection.

Performance is something everyone is concerned about. When you compare antivirus programs, you will want to pick something that is capable of scanning your system quickly, if you don't want to wait for too long when scanning your system. More important, you will definitely want a program that doesn't have a huge impact on your computer's performance. Look at the performance ratings and compare them, to find a well-balanced solution. A slightly slower scanning speed is a good trade-off, if it means that you won't have to deal with slow file transfers, unresponsive controls or choppy frame rates in your games.

With detection and performance out of the way, it's time to compare the extra features you can expect from each antivirus. Parental control is good to have, if you have children who want to play on your computer. Compare all products and look for something with a built-in parental advisor, if you want to make sure your children won't access violent or adult content or damage your system by mistake. Furthermore, online banking protection is becoming more and more important these days. Compare the products carefully, because not all programs come with such a feature. Choose a program with a safe online banking feature, if you want to get rid of that tingling feeling of unease, each time you input your credit card details on a website, to buy something.

Modern antivirus programs don't stop at protecting the operating system and your files against virus attacks. Compare the privacy-oriented features they have to offer and decide which antivirus can suit your needs the best. If you feel the need to place a piece of tape on your webcam's lens, then pick an antivirus that comes with a webcam protection feature. You may also want to protect other devices, like laptops, smartphones or tablets, so look for an antivirus that is available for multiple platforms. While most products are available for various devices, you should still compare the features they have to offer. One product may offer anti-theft functionality, but if you are more interested in keeping your device in good shape, then look for something with a battery mode, which can prolong your device's battery life.
